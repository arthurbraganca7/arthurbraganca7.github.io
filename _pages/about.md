I am a senior economist at [The World Bank Environment Department](https://www.worldbank.org/en/topic/environment) working at the intersection of research and policy in environment, climate, and agriculture. 

I work with governments across Latin America to design, finance, and implement policies on forest conservation and restoration, sustainable agriculture, and climate and nature finance. I also produce technical work, from policy reports to academic papers, that informs these efforts. My research has been published in journals such as [PNAS](https://www.pnas.org/), [Journal of Public Economics](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-public-economics), and [Journal of Development Economics](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-development-economics).

My complete CV is available [here](http://arthurbraganca7.github.io/files/CV_Arthur.pdf).
